WE ARE VENOM

Explore the terrifying and thrilling world of the Lethal Protector, one of Marvel's most iconic anti-heroes.

Origin Story

The story of Venom begins with an alien symbiote, a sentient extraterrestrial organism, that first bonded with Spider-Man during the Secret Wars storyline. Initially enhancing his abilities, the symbiote was rejected by Peter Parker due to its increasingly aggressive and possessive nature.

It then sought out a new host, finding one in Eddie Brock, a disgraced journalist who harbored a deep resentment towards Peter Parker (both as Spider-Man and in his civilian identity). The symbiote, sensing Brock's hatred, bonded with him, creating a being driven by a singular purpose: to torment and destroy Spider-Man. This union of a powerful alien entity and a vengeful human formed Venom, a dark reflection of Spider-Man with enhanced abilities and a menacing persona.

Powers & Abilities

The Venom symbiote grants its host a formidable array of powers, making them one of the most dangerous entities in the Marvel Universe:

  • Superhuman Strength & Durability: Far exceeding Spider-Man's, capable of lifting several tons and withstanding heavy impacts.
  • Enhanced Agility & Reflexes: Though less agile than Spider-Man, Venom possesses peak human to superhuman levels of speed and reflexes.
  • Shapeshifting & Camouflage: The symbiote can alter its form, mimic clothing, and blend into surroundings, making the host virtually invisible.
  • Regenerative Healing Factor: Rapidly heals from injuries that would be fatal to normal humans.
  • Web-like Constructs: Can generate organic webbing from its body, stronger than Spider-Man's synthetic webs.
  • Immunity to Spider-Sense: Due to its prior bond with Peter Parker, Venom is undetectable by Spider-Man's precognitive Spider-Sense.
  • Wall-Crawling: Similar to Spider-Man, can cling to surfaces.
  • Fangs and Claws: Can manifest razor-sharp fangs and claws for combat.

Notable Appearances

Venom has terrorized and sometimes protected the Marvel Universe across various media:

In Comics:

  • The Amazing Spider-Man #300 (1988): Marks the full debut of Eddie Brock as Venom.
  • Venom: Lethal Protector (1993): The first solo Venom mini-series, establishing him as an anti-hero.
  • Maximum Carnage (1993): A major crossover event featuring Venom, Spider-Man, and Carnage.
  • Venom (2011 series): Flash Thompson takes on the mantle of Agent Venom, working for the U.S. government.
  • King in Black (2020-2021): A massive event where Knull, the Symbiote God, invades Earth, with Venom at the center.

In Movies:

  • Spider-Man 3 (2007): Topher Grace portrays Eddie Brock/Venom in his first live-action film appearance.
  • Venom (2018): Tom Hardy stars as Eddie Brock, with the symbiote voiced by himself. The film explores their anti-hero dynamic.
  • Venom: Let There Be Carnage (2021): Tom Hardy reprises his role, facing off against Cletus Kasady/Carnage.
  • Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021): Features a post-credits scene cameo, hinting at future integration.
