Our Story

Founded by cat lovers for cat lovers, Purrfect Brew Café is more than just a coffee shop – it's a haven where humans and felines connect. We offer a cozy atmosphere, ethically sourced coffee, and a chance to interact with adorable, often adoptable, cats. Our mission is to provide a unique café experience while promoting feline welfare and adoption.

Cat Zone Rules

For the safety and comfort of our cats and guests:

  • 1. Supervise children at all times.
  • 2. No flash photography.
  • 3. Do not pick up cats.
  • 4. Wash hands before and after interaction.
  • 5. Respect quiet cats and sleeping cats.
