CSS Flexbox: A Powerful Layout Module

Published on January 05, 2024

Flexbox, or the Flexible Box Module, is a one-dimensional layout method for arranging items in rows or columns. Items flex (grow or shrink) to fill the extra space or shrink to fit into smaller spaces. It's incredibly powerful for distributing space among items in a container, and aligning them, making complex layouts much easier to achieve.
